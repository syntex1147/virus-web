A New Virus Surfaces, Some are calling it The Next Covid

President Joe Biden has been reported with an infection of Ligma, a new virus whilst abroad in Taiwan for negotiations on the current trade war.

The acclaimed new virus is rumored to be from Taiwan. This disease is ravaging parts of Western Asia and is being called the very next COVID. Scientists have named it Ligma (ligmaviridae). It has been proven to be very viral, spreading through airborne droplets (coughing and sneezing) and through touch. Symptoms may include hypoxia, fever, and profuse sweating. The CDC has stated that to keep it from spreading it has become mandatory to stay inside unless for extreme emergencies. It has also been stated that Emmanuel Macron has also contracted the virus but the French government has been very secretive about it. The CDC has said that people of all ages are at risk and if you really need to go outside you must wear a mask and gloves to protect yourself from the virus.
